✦ Core Mechanics

  • Draw edges between grid dots to form one continuous, non-intersecting loop obeying number constraints (0–4 per cell)
  • Constraint propagation engine that auto-eliminates impossible edges and highlights forced moves
  • Procedural puzzle generator producing unique, solvable boards from 5×5 (Easy) up to 15×15 (Expert)
  • Real-time validation that detects illegal branches, premature closures, and constraint violations
  • Progressive difficulty system: Easy → Medium → Hard → Expert, scaling grid size and reducing given clues
  • Undo/redo stack, hint system, and timer-based scoring for competitive play
💡

Why this matters: Implements graph traversal, constraint satisfaction (CSP), and procedural content generation — core techniques used in level design tools, pathfinding systems, and puzzle mechanics across AAA and indie titles.
